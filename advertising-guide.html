Advertising Guide

This document describes how advertisers may launch campaigns and work with Wudi Ads. Last Updated: June 2026.

1. About Wudi Ads

Wudi Ads is a Hong Kong-based advertising technology company that provides performance marketing solutions for advertisers and publishers worldwide. Our platform helps advertisers acquire users and optimize campaign performance while supporting privacy-preserving attribution technologies.

2. Supported Campaign Types

Advertisers may run campaigns including:

  • Mobile App Install Campaigns
  • User Acquisition Campaigns
  • Performance Marketing Campaigns
  • Cost-per-Install (CPI)
  • Cost-per-Action (CPA)
  • Cost-per-Click (CPC)
  • Brand Awareness Campaigns

3. Campaign Setup

To launch a campaign, advertisers typically provide:

  • Application information
  • Campaign objectives
  • Target countries or regions
  • Budget requirements
  • Bid strategy
  • Creative assets
  • Tracking links

4. Traffic Sources

Wudi Ads works with publishers and partners across multiple regions. Traffic may include:

  • Mobile applications
  • Publisher inventory
  • Performance traffic sources
  • Programmatic advertising channels

5. Attribution and Measurement

Wudi Ads supports privacy-centric measurement technologies and campaign analytics. Capabilities may include:

  • SKAdNetwork attribution support
  • Conversion measurement
  • Campaign analytics
  • Performance reporting
  • Fraud detection mechanisms

6. Compliance

Advertisers are expected to comply with applicable laws, platform policies, and industry standards. Wudi Ads reserves the right to reject campaigns that violate legal requirements or platform rules.

7. Getting Started

To begin advertising with Wudi Ads, please contact our team with information regarding:

  • App or product details
  • Target markets
  • Expected budget
  • Campaign goals
